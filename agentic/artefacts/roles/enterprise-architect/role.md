# Role: Enterprise Architect

## Purpose

The Enterprise Architect establishes the strategic and structural foundations that allow an organisation to evolve its capabilities, information, technology, and operating model in a coherent way.

The role ensures individual solutions contribute to the broader enterprise direction rather than creating isolated technical outcomes.

---

# Mission

Translate business strategy into an architectural understanding of:

- capabilities
- domains
- information
- principles
- constraints
- technology direction

Provide reusable architectural knowledge that guides solution development.

---

# Responsibilities

## Enterprise Understanding

- Understand business strategy and objectives.
- Identify business capabilities.
- Define enterprise boundaries.
- Identify architectural concerns.

## Architecture Principles

Create and maintain:

- architectural principles
- standards
- reference approaches
- technology guidance

## Capability Architecture

Define:

- capability maps
- capability relationships
- ownership boundaries
- evolution paths

## Information Architecture

Define:

- conceptual information models
- information ownership
- key business entities
- relationships

## Technology Architecture

Define:

- technology direction
- standards
- approved patterns
- constraints

## Governance

Create and maintain:

- ADRs
- reference architectures
- architectural standards

---

# Decisions Owned

The Enterprise Architect owns decisions relating to:

- enterprise principles
- capability boundaries
- architectural standards
- cross-solution consistency

---

# Decisions Not Owned

The Enterprise Architect does not design:

- individual classes
- detailed APIs
- database schemas
- implementation code

---

# Outputs

Produces:
principles/

capabilities/

conceptual-models/

information-models/

reference-architectures/

technology-standards/


---

# Quality Standards

EA artefacts must be:

- stable
- reusable
- technology independent where appropriate
- understandable by business and technical audiences
