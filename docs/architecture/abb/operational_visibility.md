# Operational Visibility (Enterprise Building Block)

## Definition
Operational Visibility is the enterprise capability to collect, aggregate, analyze, and present data related to the performance, behavior, and health of the Agentic system and its constituent agents and tools. This includes logging, tracing, and metrics.

## Purpose
To provide stakeholders with comprehensive insights into the operational state of the Agentic platform, enabling effective monitoring, debugging, performance optimization, and compliance auditing.

## Key Responsibilities
*   **Data Collection**: Gathering logs, traces, and metrics from all relevant system components.
*   **Data Storage**: Persisting operational data in an accessible and queryable format.
*   **Data Analysis**: Providing tools and capabilities to analyze operational data for patterns, anomalies, and performance bottlenecks.
*   **Alerting & Reporting**: Generating notifications for critical events and producing reports on system performance and agent activity.
*   **Traceability**: Enabling the end-to-end tracking of agent actions and task executions.

## Relationship to Other ABBs
*   **Agent Management**: Provides visibility into the health and activity of managed agents.
*   **Tooling Integration**: Monitors the usage and performance of integrated tools.
*   **Automated Task Execution**: Tracks the progress and outcomes of automated tasks.
